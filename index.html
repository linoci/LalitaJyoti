About Us

Lalita Jyoti is a social enterprise based on community empowerment. We aim to address the growing demand for sustainable fashion by utilizing pineapple leaf fiber, an eco-friendly, biodegradable material. Our mission is to create a positive environmental and social impact by supporting local communities and promoting the use of renewable resources.

Our Story

Lalita Jyoti was founded with a vision to empower pineapple farmers by transforming agricultural waste into a source of income. Recognizing the detrimental effects of chemical pollution caused by fast fashion industries, particularly in Solo, Central Java, Indonesia, we sought to create an eco-friendly solution. By using pineapple leaf fiber, a sustainable and biodegradable material, we not only provide an alternative income for farmers but also contribute to reducing environmental pollution. Our commitment to sustainability drives every aspect of our business, from production to global market reach.

Our Vision & Mission

Discover our purpose and goals for a sustainable future.

Vision



To lead the global shift towards sustainable fashion by transforming agricultural waste into stylish, durable, and eco-friendly products, empowering communities, and fostering environmental stewardship.

Mission

  • 1. Empowerment: To uplift local farmers, women, and people with disabilities by providing them with sustainable income opportunities through the creation of high-quality fashion and accessories made from pineapple leaf fiber.
  • 2. Sustainability: To address the environmental impact of the fashion industry by offering products that are both stylish and environmentally friendly, reducing waste, and promoting the use of biodegradable materials.
  • 3. Global Impact: To create a global brand that champions sustainability, promotes ethical production, and inspires consumers worldwide to make environmentally conscious choices in fashion.

Partnership

Lalita Jyoti has partnered with 1 Regency, namely Siak Regency to conduct farmer training in pineapple fiber processing. Through this collaboration, it is hoped that farmers can improve their skills and productivity in producing high-quality pineapple fiber that is ready to be further processed into products with economic value.

Sustainability Impact

100+ Women & People with Disabilities employed in crafting our products, earning a fair and sustainable livelihood.

We collaborate with rural pineapple farmers, women, and people with disabilities to transform agricultural waste into high-quality products.

Location

We collaborate with pineapple farming communities in Karanganyar Regency, Central Java, and Siak Regency, Riau. These farmers are key partners in supplying pineapple leaves as the primary raw material for our eco-friendly fabrics. This partnership supports local economic empowerment while promoting sustainability. Together, we create innovative textile products that positively impact the environment.
